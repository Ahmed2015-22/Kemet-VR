The Great Architecture

Building for Eternity

Ancient Egyptian architecture is the architecture of ancient Egypt, one of the most influential civilizations throughout history.

They developed a vast array of diverse structures and great architectural monuments along the Nile, including pyramids and temples. Due to the scarcity of wood, the two predominant building materials used in ancient Egypt were sun-baked mud brick and stone, mainly limestone.
