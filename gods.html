The Pantheon of Gods

Ra

The Sun God. He was believed to rule in all parts of the created world: the sky, the earth, and the underworld.

Osiris

The God of the Underworld and the afterlife, agriculture, and the dead. Husband of Isis.

Anubis

The jackal-headed God of mummification and the afterlife. He guided souls to the hall of truth.
